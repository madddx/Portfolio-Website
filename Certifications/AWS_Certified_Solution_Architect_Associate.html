Overview

About This Certification

I earned the AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate certification, which validates my ability to design secure, scalable, and reliable cloud solutions using Amazon Web Services (AWS). This certification strengthened my understanding of cloud architecture principles and modern infrastructure design.

Through this certification, I gained practical knowledge in designing highly available systems, managing networking and storage services, and implementing cost-effective cloud solutions. It also enhanced my ability to build architectures that meet performance, security, and operational requirements.

The credential helped me develop expertise in selecting the right AWS services for different business and technical use cases. I also explored best practices for cloud security, fault tolerance, monitoring, and workload optimization within real-world AWS environments.

Achieving this certification reflects my commitment to advancing my cloud engineering and architectural skills. It has strengthened my confidence in designing efficient cloud infrastructures and delivering scalable solutions that support modern application development and business growth.

Certification Domains

AWS Solutions Architect Knowledge Areas

Domain 1

Design Secure Architectures

Designing secure AWS environments using identity management, encryption, network security, and best practices to protect applications, workloads, and cloud resources.

Domain 2

Design Resilient Architectures

Building highly available and fault-tolerant systems capable of handling failures, ensuring business continuity, and maintaining reliable cloud operations.

Domain 3

Design High-Performing Architectures

Optimising compute, storage, databases, and networking services to deliver scalable, efficient, and high-performance cloud applications.

Domain 4

Design Cost-Optimised Architectures

Applying AWS pricing strategies, resource optimisation, and architectural best practices to balance performance, scalability, and operational cost efficiency.

Domain 5

Architectural Best Practices

Implementing AWS Well-Architected principles, modern deployment strategies, and cloud-native patterns for reliable and scalable enterprise solutions.
